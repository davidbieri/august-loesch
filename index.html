This project aims to create a consolidated, comprehensive online archive of the collected papers of the German economist and spatial theorist Karl Wilhelm August Lösch (1906-1945), among whose most influential teachers and life-long mentors were the great German economists Walter Eucken (1891-1950), Joseph Schumpeter (1883-1950), and Arthur Spiethoff (1873-1957).

Key features of the online archive include: (i) digitized manuscripts (including hitherto unpublished works) and collected papers; (ii) transcribed letters and other correspondence; (iii) selected English translations of selected works; and (iv) original data sets collected by Lösch (firm-level micro data from the 1930s, historical macro time series for Germany and the US from 1870s to 1940).
